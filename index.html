About Us

PT Karunia Tani Utama

PT Karunia Tani Utama is a company involved in trading high-quality agricultural products for local or global markets. As an enthusiastic and dedicated company, we focus on connecting outstanding harvests from fertile fields.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the main actor in promoting Indonesian agricultural sustainability and reconciling natural resources with global needs

Our Mission
  • Provide global needs with best quality product
  • Encourage local farmer go to global market
  • Provide the Best quality product and service
  • Provide provesionality and good integrity to our customer
